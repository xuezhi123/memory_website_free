Dear friend,

This is a placeholder article to demonstrate the memorial article feature.

Replace the files in `src/content/articles/` with real stories, letters, and memories.

Each article supports:

- **Markdown formatting** (bold, italic, lists)
- **Dates** for chronological ordering
- **Excerpts** shown in the article list
- **Font size controls** (S / M / L) on the article detail page

To add a new article, simply create a `.md` file in `src/content/articles/` with frontmatter like this:

```yaml
---
title: "Your Article Title"
date: "2024-01-15"
excerpt: "A short summary shown in the list."
---

Your article content here...
```
